Cookies

Description

Easy to make cookies

Ingredients

  • 1 cup oats (100 g)
  • 2 bananas

Steps

  1. Step 1

    2. Preheat oven to 350˚F (180˚C).

  2. Step 2

    3. In a large mixing bowl combine the oats and banana and mash banana and mix until well incorporated.

  3. Step 3

    4. Add additional ingredients should you desire.

  4. Step 4

    5. Bake for 12 minutes and enjoy!
